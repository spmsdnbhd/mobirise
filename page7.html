ABOUT US

Background of business

Syarikat Perniagaan Melayu Sdn Bhd (SPM Sdn Bhd) before this known as Syarikat Perniagaan Melayu Kuala Belait (SPMKB) are build and registered in year 1966. It's officially change name SPMKB to SPM Sdn Bhd on 22nd April 2014.

Our business are located at Lot 3770, Jalan Panglima/Jalan Sungai Pandan, Kuala Belait for business purpose. (Fueling Station and Service).

SPM Sdn Bhd selling fuel on behalf of Shell Marketing Co. Ltd Brunei which official launch by YAM Pengiran Dipa Negara Pengiran Pengiran Abd Momin Pengiran Haji Ismail on Sunday, 02nd February 1969.
